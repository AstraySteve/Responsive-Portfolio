About Me

My name is Steven Tran, and I'm currently looking for a career in the direction of a full-stack web developer. I have studied at the University of Toronto with a major in computer-science and have used the following programming languages: Python, Java, JavaScript, C, C# and C++. I am looking forward to reinforcing my knowledge and building a presentable portfolio at the UofT SCS Coding Bootcamp.


Aside from working and playing around with computers and programming, my core skillset and interest revolves around being able to create things. My hobbies involves making replica props from various media and building scaled plastic or 'paper' (cardstock) models. With this said, I have a future interest in a branching career path either into the gaming or animation industries.


Fun Fact: My other hobby is archery, and I can't wait for the weather to be good enough to hit the range again.
